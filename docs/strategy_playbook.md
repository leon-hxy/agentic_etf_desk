# Strategy Playbook

## Version 1 Universe

Only ETFs are eligible. Individual stocks, options, futures, crypto assets, leveraged ETFs, and inverse ETFs are excluded unless a future explicit allowlist is added.

## Required Benchmarking

Every strategy must compare against an appropriate benchmark before it can appear in a report or recommendation ticket.

Initial benchmark candidates:

- Buy and hold benchmark.
- Static 60/40 benchmark.
- Equal-weight ETF benchmark.

## Initial Strategy Templates

- Benchmark buy and hold.
- Static 60/40.
- Equal-weight ETF.
- GTAA 10-month simple moving average.
- Dual momentum.
- Time-series momentum with volatility target.
- Inverse-volatility allocation.
- ETF mean-reversion sandbox.

## Risk Review

Every trade recommendation ticket must pass risk review. Risk review should cover:

- ETF eligibility.
- Concentration.
- Drawdown.
- Volatility.
- Turnover.
- Data freshness.
- Benchmark comparison.
- Manual-trading disclaimer.

## Ticket Disclaimer

Every recommendation ticket must include:

> This is research advice, not automatic order placement. Final trading is manually decided by the user.
